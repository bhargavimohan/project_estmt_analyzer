Welcome to E-statement Analyzer

"E-Statement Analyzer" is a user-friendly app that simplifies financial management by automatically categorizing your credit card expenses from PDF statements. It breaks down your spending into categories like groceries, medical costs, and more, helping you understand your monthly expenses at a glance. Plus, it verifies your expenses against the provided new balance, ensuring accuracy and helping you spot any discrepancies.
